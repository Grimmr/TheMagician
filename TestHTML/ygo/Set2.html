The Yu-Gi-Oh! The Eternal Duelist Soul promotional cards are a set of 3 cards included with the video game Yu-Gi-Oh! The Eternal Duelist Soul.

These cards were later released as the European English, French, German, and Italian versions of the Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards promotional cards.

Breakdown[edit]

  • 2 Spell Cards
  • 1 Trap Cards
  • 3 Prismatic Secret Rares
